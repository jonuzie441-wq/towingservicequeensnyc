When Do You Need Emergency Towing in Queens?

There are many situations where emergency towing becomes necessary. Understanding when to call for a tow truck can save you time, money, and stress. Here are the most common emergency towing scenarios we handle throughout Queens:

  • Mechanical breakdowns — Engine failure, transmission problems, overheating, and other mechanical issues that leave your vehicle undrivable on Queens roads.
  • Highway breakdowns — Stalling on the BQE, LIE, Van Wyck, Grand Central Parkway, or Cross Island Parkway where staying in your vehicle can be dangerous.
  • Accident recovery — After a collision, your vehicle may need to be towed to a body shop or impound lot for insurance processing.
  • Flat tires without a spare — If your tire blows out and you do not have a spare or the spare is also flat, a tow to the nearest tire shop is the safest option.
  • Electrical failures — Complete electrical system failures that prevent your car from starting or operating safely.
  • Flooding and weather damage — Queens is prone to flash flooding in low-lying areas. If your vehicle stalls in standing water, emergency towing is critical.
