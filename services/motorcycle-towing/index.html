Specialized Motorcycle Towing Across Queens

Motorcycles require a completely different approach to towing than cars and trucks. A standard tow truck hookup can damage your bike's frame, scratch its bodywork, or stress critical components. That is why Towing Service Queens NYC uses specialized motorcycle towing equipment and techniques designed specifically for two-wheeled vehicles. Our flatbed tow trucks are fitted with motorcycle wheel chocks, soft-loop tie-down straps, and rubber-padded cradles that secure your bike firmly without any metal-to-metal contact. From the moment we load your motorcycle to the moment we deliver it, every precaution is taken to ensure zero damage.

Whether you ride a sport bike, a Harley-Davidson cruiser, a touring motorcycle, an adventure bike, or a custom chopper, our operators have the experience and equipment to handle your ride with the respect it deserves. When Motorcycle Towing Is Needed

There are several common situations where you might need motorcycle towing services in Queens:

  • Mechanical breakdowns — Engine failure, electrical problems, chain or belt issues, or other mechanical faults that leave your bike unrideable.
  • Flat tires — Unlike cars, motorcycles rarely carry spare tires. A flat tire on a Queens street means you need a tow.
  • Accident recovery — After a motorcycle accident, safe recovery and transport by a professional tow service is critical.
  • Dead battery — If a jump start does not work, your motorcycle needs to be transported to a shop for battery replacement or electrical diagnosis.
  • Planned transport — Moving your motorcycle to a new location, a dealership, a storage facility, or a track day event.
  • Seasonal storage — Queens riders often store their motorcycles for winter and need reliable transport to and from storage facilities.
  • New purchases — Picking up a motorcycle you bought from a private seller or online listing anywhere in the area.

Our Motorcycle Towing Process and Equipment

When you call us for motorcycle towing, here is what happens. Our dispatcher confirms your location, motorcycle type, and destination. We send the nearest flatbed truck equipped with motorcycle-specific gear. Upon arrival, our operator positions the flatbed with the bed tilted to its lowest angle for easy loading. Your motorcycle is carefully rolled or carried onto the bed and placed in a wheel chock that holds the front tire securely in place. Soft-loop straps are then attached to the handlebars, frame, or footpegs — never to painted surfaces or fragile components. The bike is compressed slightly into its suspension to create tension, and additional rear straps are attached to prevent any lateral movement. The entire process takes about ten minutes, and your bike is completely secure for transport.
