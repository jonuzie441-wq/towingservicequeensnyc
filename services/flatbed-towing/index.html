The Safest Way to Transport Your Vehicle in Queens

Flatbed towing is widely recognized as the gold standard in vehicle transport. Unlike traditional hook-and-chain or wheel-lift methods, flatbed towing places your entire vehicle on a flat, hydraulic platform that tilts to ground level for loading. Once your car is securely on the bed and strapped down, it rides with all four wheels off the ground, completely eliminating any risk of tire wear, transmission damage, or drivetrain stress during transport. For vehicle owners in Queens who demand the highest level of care for their automobiles, flatbed towing from Towing Service Queens NYC is the clear choice.

Our flatbed tow trucks are maintained to the highest standards and operated by experienced drivers who specialize in damage-free vehicle transport. Whether you need to move a brand-new sports car from a dealership in Bayside, transport a broken-down SUV from Astoria to your mechanic, or relocate a classic car from a garage in Forest Hills, our flatbed service ensures your vehicle arrives in the exact same condition it was loaded in.

When Flatbed Towing Is the Right Choice

While any vehicle can benefit from flatbed towing, certain situations make it essential rather than optional. If your vehicle falls into any of the following categories, flatbed towing is strongly recommended:

  • All-wheel drive (AWD) vehicles — Towing AWD vehicles with wheels on the ground can cause severe drivetrain damage costing thousands in repairs.
  • Luxury and exotic cars — Vehicles like BMW, Mercedes, Porsche, Ferrari, and Lamborghini require the protective transport only a flatbed provides.
  • Lowered or modified vehicles — Cars with low ground clearance, body kits, or aftermarket suspension cannot be safely loaded with a wheel-lift truck.
  • Non-running vehicles — If your car cannot be put in neutral or has locked-up wheels, a flatbed is the only safe towing option.
  • Vehicles with flat tires — Multiple flat tires make wheel-based towing impossible, but a flatbed handles it with ease.
  • Classic and collectible cars — Vintage vehicles deserve the utmost care during transport, and flatbed towing delivers that protection.
  • Accident-damaged vehicles — After a collision, the extent of mechanical damage may be unknown, making flatbed towing the safest option.

Frequently Asked Questions About Flatbed Towing

Why should I choose flatbed towing over regular towing?
Flatbed towing is the safest method because your entire vehicle rests on the truck bed, eliminating wear on tires, transmission, and drivetrain. It's essential for AWD vehicles, luxury cars, lowered vehicles, and any car that cannot roll safely. There is zero road contact during transport.
Can you flatbed tow an AWD or 4WD vehicle in Queens?
Yes, flatbed towing is the recommended method for AWD and 4WD vehicles. Towing these vehicles with wheels on the ground can damage the drivetrain. Our flatbed trucks lift the entire vehicle off the ground for completely safe transport anywhere in Queens.
