All-wheel drive and four-wheel drive vehicles cannot be safely towed with standard wheel lift methods because doing so can damage the drivetrain, transfer case, or transmission. Dolly towing solves this problem by placing a two-wheel dolly under the front wheels while the rear wheels sit on the tow truck. This keeps all four wheels either on the dolly or on the truck, eliminating any drivetrain engagement.
