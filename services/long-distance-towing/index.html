Trusted Long Distance Towing From Queens, NY

Sometimes your vehicle needs to travel farther than just across town. Whether you are relocating to another state, transporting a vehicle you purchased online, or need a broken-down car moved to a specialist mechanic outside of Queens, Towing Service Queens NYC provides reliable long distance towing that covers the entire East Coast and beyond. Our long distance towing service operates on modern flatbed trucks that keep your vehicle fully protected over hundreds of miles, with experienced drivers who are familiar with interstate routes and know how to deliver your vehicle safely and on time.

Long distance towing requires a different level of preparation and execution than a local tow. The vehicle must be secured more thoroughly for highway speeds, the driver must plan for fuel stops and rest breaks on longer journeys, and communication with the customer must be consistent throughout the trip. At Towing Service Queens NYC, we take all of these factors seriously. When you trust us with a long distance tow, you can track the progress and expect your vehicle at the estimated arrival time.

  • Specialist repair shops — Transporting a vehicle to a specialized mechanic or body shop outside the Queens area.

  • Dealership transfers — Moving vehicles between dealership locations across state lines.
  • Breakdown on a trip — If your car breaks down while traveling and you want it brought back to your trusted Queens mechanic.
  • Snowbird seasonal moves — Transporting vehicles to Florida or southern states for the winter season.

Our drivers are long-haul experienced and hold all required commercial driving licenses. They plan routes to avoid construction zones and heavy traffic when possible, and they maintain communication with our dispatch team and with you throughout the trip. GPS tracking is available so you know exactly where your vehicle is at any point during the journey.

Frequently Asked Questions About Long Distance Towing

How far can you tow my vehicle from Queens?
We offer long distance towing from Queens to anywhere in the continental United States. Most of our long distance tows go to destinations in New York State, New Jersey, Connecticut, Pennsylvania, Massachusetts, and other East Coast states, but we can tow cross-country if needed.
How much does long distance towing from Queens cost?
Long distance towing is priced per mile plus a base hookup fee. Rates vary based on vehicle type and destination. We offer competitive per-mile pricing and provide free quotes over the phone. Call (347) 437-0185 for an exact price to your destination.
Do you use flatbed trucks for long distance towing?
Yes, all our long distance tows are performed using flatbed trucks to ensure maximum safety over extended distances. This protects your vehicle from road debris, tire wear, and drivetrain damage throughout the entire journey.
How long does a long distance tow take?
Travel time depends on the destination distance and traffic conditions. We provide estimated arrival times when you book. For example, a tow from Queens to Philadelphia takes approximately 2-3 hours, while a tow to Boston takes about 4-5 hours.
