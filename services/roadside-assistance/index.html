When to Call for Roadside Assistance vs. a Tow

If your issue is something that can potentially be fixed on site, roadside assistance is the faster and more affordable option. Dead batteries, flat tires, empty tanks, and lockouts are almost always resolved by our roadside team without needing a tow. However, if the problem is more serious — engine failure, transmission issues, major electrical problems, or accident damage — a tow to a repair shop is the better choice. When you call us, our dispatcher will help determine whether roadside assistance or towing is the right solution for your situation. Either way, we have you covered.
