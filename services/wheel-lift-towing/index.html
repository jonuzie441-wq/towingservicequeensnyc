Professional Wheel Lift Towing Services Throughout Queens, NY

Wheel lift towing is the classic and most common form of tow truck service. Instead of loading a vehicle onto a flatbed, a hydraulic yoke lifts the vehicle's drive wheels off the ground while the non-drive wheels roll on the pavement. This method is fast, efficient, and works well for most standard vehicles that are still operational or recently disabled.

Our Wheel Lift Towing Equipment and Process

Professional Equipment: Wheel lift wreckers, hydraulic yokes, L-arms, soft tow bars, safety chains, independent wheel lifts with lateral movement capability. We invest in top-tier equipment because we know that quality tools prevent damage, speed up service, and ensure safety. Every piece of equipment is inspected before each shift and maintained on a strict schedule.

Step by Step Process: Our wheel lift truck backs up to your vehicle, the hydraulic yoke extends and grabs your drive wheels, lifts them off the ground, secures them with safety chains, and tows your vehicle to its destination with the non-drive wheels rolling. This process has been refined over thousands of calls and represents the fastest, safest way to handle wheel lift towing situations. Our drivers follow this process every time, regardless of the complexity of the job.

Vehicle Types We Handle: Front-wheel drive cars, rear-wheel drive cars, most sedans, SUVs with front-wheel drive. No vehicle is too big, too small, too old, or too specialized for our team. If it's a vehicle, we can provide wheel lift towing for it.
