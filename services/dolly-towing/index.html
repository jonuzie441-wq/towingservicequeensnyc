Dolly Towing Service in Queens, New York

All-wheel drive and four-wheel drive vehicles require special towing methods to prevent drivetrain damage. Our dolly towing service uses towing dollies to lift the non-towed wheels off the ground, preventing damage to differentials, transfer cases, and transmissions. This is essential for AWD vehicles like Subarus, Audis, and many modern SUVs.

At Towing Service Queens NYC, we provide professional dolly towing service across every neighborhood in Queens. Based at 15557 Bayview Ave, our team is strategically positioned to reach you quickly — with average response times of just 15-30 minutes to any location in the borough.

When You Need Dolly Towing Service

There are many situations where you might need dolly towing service in Queens:

  • AWD vehicle breakdown — Subaru, Audi, BMW xDrive
  • 4WD truck towing — Transfer case protection needed
  • No flatbed available — Dolly is next safest option
  • Short-distance AWD tow — Cost-effective alternative to flatbed
  • Parking lot relocation — Moving AWD vehicles safely

Our Equipment & Process

We use professional-grade tow dollies that securely lift and cradle either the front or rear wheels of your vehicle. Combined with our wheel-lift or hook-style tow trucks, the dolly keeps all four wheels off the ground during transport — the safest method for AWD and 4WD vehicles when a flatbed is not available.

Our operators are trained professionals who handle every situation with care and expertise. We use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure your vehicle is handled safely throughout the entire process.
