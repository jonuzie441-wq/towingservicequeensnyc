When You Need Heavy Duty Towing

Heavy duty towing is required whenever a vehicle exceeds the capacity of standard tow trucks, typically anything over 10,000 pounds gross vehicle weight. Common situations requiring heavy duty towing in Queens include:

  • Commercial truck breakdowns — Box trucks, delivery trucks, and semi-trucks that experience mechanical failure on Queens roads and highways.
  • Bus breakdowns and recovery — MTA buses, charter buses, school buses, and shuttle buses that need towing to maintenance facilities.
  • RV and motorhome towing — Large recreational vehicles that break down while passing through or visiting Queens.
  • Construction vehicle transport — Moving heavy equipment like excavators, loaders, and cranes between job sites.
  • Accident recovery for large vehicles — Post-accident towing of trucks and commercial vehicles, often involving cargo management.
  • Disabled garbage and utility trucks — Municipal and private service vehicles that need emergency towing.
