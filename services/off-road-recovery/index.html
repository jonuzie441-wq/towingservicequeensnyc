When You Need Off-Road Recovery Service

There are many situations where you might need off-road recovery service in Queens:

  • Stuck in mud or dirt — Construction site or unpaved area
  • Beach sand stuck — Vehicle stuck at Rockaway beaches
  • Park or grass stuck — Driven onto soft ground

Our Equipment & Process

We deploy trucks equipped with heavy-duty winches, extended-reach booms, and off-road recovery gear including snatch straps, tree savers, D-rings, and traction mats. For deep recovery situations, we can use multiple winch points and mechanical advantage systems to safely extract even heavily stuck vehicles.

Our operators are trained professionals who handle every situation with care and expertise. We use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure your vehicle is handled safely throughout the entire process.
