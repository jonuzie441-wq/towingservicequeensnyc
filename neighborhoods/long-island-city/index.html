Professional Towing Service in Long Island City, Queens

Long Island City — universally known as "LIC" — has transformed from an industrial waterfront into one of New York City's most dynamic neighborhoods. With its glittering high-rise towers along the East River, thriving arts scene anchored by MoMA PS1 and the Sculpture Center, and proximity to Midtown Manhattan via the Queensboro Bridge and the 7 train, LIC attracts residents, commuters, and visitors around the clock. This constant activity means vehicle trouble can happen anytime, anywhere in the neighborhood.

Towing Service Queens NYC delivers fast, dependable towing and roadside assistance throughout Long Island City, covering ZIP codes 11101 and 11109. From the bustling Queens Plaza transit hub to the serene Gantry Plaza State Park waterfront, our tow trucks are ready to respond in 10 to 20 minutes.

Navigating LIC's Unique Towing Challenges

Long Island City's road network presents distinct challenges. The Queens Boulevard and Jackson Avenue corridors carry enormous volumes of traffic feeding into the Queensboro Bridge and Queens-Midtown Tunnel approaches. During rush hour, a stalled vehicle on Queens Plaza or at the intersection of Jackson Avenue and Thomson Avenue can cause major backups — and you need that vehicle moved fast.

The waterfront area along Center Boulevard and 46th Avenue features narrow access roads between towering residential buildings. Parking garages beneath these buildings are often tight, and vehicles occasionally need towing from underground levels. Our operators have extensive experience navigating these spaces with the right equipment.

Vernon Boulevard, LIC's main north-south artery, connects the neighborhood from Hunters Point to Ravenswood. Its mix of restaurants, shops, and residential traffic makes it a frequent site for lockouts and minor fender-benders. Meanwhile, the industrial blocks around Review Avenue and Skillman Avenue still see heavy commercial vehicle traffic, creating demand for our heavy duty towing capabilities.
