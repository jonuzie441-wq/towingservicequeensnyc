About Towing Service Queens NYC

Queens' most trusted locally owned towing company. Licensed, insured, and committed to fast, reliable service across every neighborhood.

Queens' Locally Owned Towing Company

Towing Service Queens NYC is a locally owned and operated towing and roadside assistance company based at 15557 Bayview Ave in Queens, New York. We have been serving the Queens community with professional, reliable, and affordable towing services around the clock.

We know Queens like the back of our hand. From the bustling streets of Flushing and Jamaica to the quiet residential blocks of Douglaston and Malba, from the waterfront developments of Long Island City to the sandy shores of Rockaway Beach — our drivers navigate every corner of this borough every single day.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: provide the fastest, most reliable, and most affordable towing and roadside assistance service in Queens, NY. When you're stranded on the side of the road at 2 AM, stuck in a ditch during a snowstorm, or locked out of your car in a parking lot, you need help fast. That's exactly what we deliver — average response times of 15-30 minutes to any Queens neighborhood.

Our Fleet & Equipment

We maintain a modern fleet of tow trucks to handle any situation:

  • Flatbed Tow Trucks — For safe, damage-free towing of all vehicles including luxury cars, AWD vehicles, and lowered vehicles
  • Wheel-Lift Tow Trucks — Quick and efficient towing for standard vehicles
  • Heavy-Duty Wreckers — For trucks, buses, RVs, construction equipment, and commercial vehicles
  • Dolly Equipment — For AWD and 4WD vehicles that need special handling
  • Winch Systems — For off-road recovery, ditch extraction, and accident recovery
  • Roadside Assistance Vehicles — Equipped for jump starts, lockouts, tire changes, and fuel delivery
